Sausage Peppers and Onions

  • chicken sausage of your liking
  • 1 white onion, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 1 red bel pepper
  • 2 TBSPs extra virgin olive oil
  • 2-3 cloves of garlic
  • fine salt
  • ground black pepper
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Meanwhile, slice onion, garlic, red and green bell peppers
  3. cut sausages into 1/2inch slices
  4. Place sausages, peppers, onions, and garlic into a foil lined sheet pan, drizzle with oil.
  5. Bake in oven for 10-15 minutes.
  6. Flip sausages and stir all the veggies
  7. Bake for another 10-15minutes
  8. Season to taste
  9. Enjoy!
