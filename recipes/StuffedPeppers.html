Stuffed Peppers

  • 4 bell peppers, any color
  • 1 medium onion, yellow, diced
  • 3 cloves of garlic, diced
  • 1 to 1.5LB ground beef, or turkey
  • 2/3's cup jasmine rice, uncooked
  • 1 can of fire roasted diced tomatoes, drained
  • 1 can of tomato sauce.
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1 TBSP extra virgin olive oil
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Ground salt
  • Ground Pepper
  • Italian seasoning
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit
  2. Grease baking dish while oven is preheating
  3. Trim tops of bell peppers and remove internal ribs and seeds
  4. Heat olive oil in a large non-stick skillet. Medium to high heat
  5. Meanwhile, dice yellow onion and garlic
  6. saute onion and garlic until translucent and set aside in pan.
  7. add in ground beef or turkey, break it up as it browns. add salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder
  8. remove from heat, drain off fat or grease
  9. stir in tomatoes, uncooked rice, italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to taste
  10. while having peppers upright fill in beef or turkey mixture, pour over some sauce and sprinkle the shredded cheese. cover lightly with foil and bake for 35minutes
  11. remove tinfoil and bake for anther 10 minutes or until cheese is browned.
  12. remove from oven and let the dish rest for 2-3 minutes. serve warm
  13. Enjoy!!
