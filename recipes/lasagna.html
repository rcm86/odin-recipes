Lasagna

  • 1-1.5LB ground beef
  • 1 sm-med yellow onion, diced
  • tomato sauce
  • egg
  • 1 cup water
  • lasagna noodles
  • ricotta, mozzarella, romano and parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • pepper
  • dried oregano
  1. preheat oven to 375 degrees fahrenheit
  2. break apart ground beef in skillet over med-high heat, until no longer pink
  3. add in diced onion, dried oregano, salt and pepper. cook through until translucent
  4. add in tomato sauce and 1 cup water to the skillet, stir and bring to boil.
  5. meanwhile, in a small bowl combine ricotta cheese, 1TBS oregano, eggs, parmesan cheese, salt and pepper. Mix it well.
  6. in a 9x13inch baking dish add a couple ladles of the sauce to the bottom.
  7. lay 3 lasagna noodles over the meat sauce. next layer with ricotta mixture and about 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  8. repeat last step about 3 to 4 times. top with ricotta and mozzarella cheese.
  9. ENJOY!
