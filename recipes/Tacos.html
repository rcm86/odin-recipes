Tacos

  • 1-1.5LB ground beef
  • 10 hard or soft tacos
  • 2/3's cup water
  • small head of lettuce, shredded
  • Onion, diced
  • Tomato, diced
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Taco sauce or salsa
  • chili powder
  • cumin
  • oregano
  • salt
  • pepper
  1. preheat oven to 325 degrees fahrenheit
  2. dice tomatoes, onion, shred lettuce and set aside
  3. Break apart ground beef in skillet over medium to high heat, stir occasionally
  4. drain excess oil and grease
  5. turn down heat, add in seasonings and water.
  6. simmer until water is evaporated but beef is still saucy. stir occasionally
  7. meanwhile, place shells in oven for 2-5 minutes and set aside. if using soft shell sear on stove top burner 1-2 min each side.
  8. spoon 2 tablespoons of beef into the hard shells or small soft tortillas
  9. top the tacos with your favorite toppings. and enjoy!
